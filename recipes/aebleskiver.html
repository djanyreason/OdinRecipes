Æbleskiver

Æbleskiver are a traditional Danish breakfast treat - kind of like pancake balls. They are best served with jam or powdered sugar.

To cook æbleskiver you need a special pan, called an æbleskiver pan. You can find many for purchase online. I use the Nordic Ware Danish Ebelskiver Pan.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/3rd cup buttermilk
  • 2/3rd cup flour
  • 1 tsp. sugar
  • 1/12th tsp. salt
  • 1/6th tsp. baking soda
  • 2 tbsp. melted butter
  • Jam or powdered sugar, to serve
