Beyond Meat Meatballs

Italian-style meatballs made out of Beyond Meat plant-based ground "meat". You can easily use Impossible Foods grounds as well, but Beyond is sold in a 1lb. package while Impossible in a 3/4 pound package, so it's not quite as easy to make 4lbs. at a time.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup Italian seasoning
  • 4 tsp. salt
  • 4 tsp. ground black peppper
  • 4 tsp. garlic powder
  • 4 lbs. Beyond Meat Plant-Based Ground Beef
  • 2 cups panko bread crumbs
