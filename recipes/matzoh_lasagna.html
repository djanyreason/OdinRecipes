Matzoh Lasagna

A traditional meat lasagna made with matzoh instead of lasagna noodles. Very tasty, and great for Chosen People who don't keep kosher but who do keep Passover kosher.

For baking, you will want a deep square oven-safe baking dish about the size of 1 sheet of matzoh (about 8.5"x8.5").

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground meat of your choice (suggestions: beef&pork mix, turkey)
  • 1 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 & 1/4 tsp. kosher salt
  • 3/8 tsp. black pepper
  • 2.5 cups marinara-based pasta sauce (I suggest something like tomato&basil or roasted garlic)
  • 1 Tbsp. Italian seasoning
  • 16oz. whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 cup shredded parmesean cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozarella cheese
  • 3 sheets matzoh
